Frequently Asked Questions

Ingredient FAQs

Learn more about the natural ingredients and craftsmanship behind every Body & Butter product.

🌿 Are your ingredients natural?

Yes. We carefully select premium botanical ingredients such as Rose Extract, Activated Charcoal, Kesar, Ubtan, and Multani Mitti to create gentle and effective skincare products.

🐰 Are your products cruelty-free?

Absolutely. We never test our products on animals and are committed to ethical, cruelty-free skincare practices.

💚 Are they suitable for sensitive skin?

Most of our products are formulated with gentle botanical ingredients suitable for daily use. However, we recommend performing a patch test before first use.

🧼 Are all soaps handcrafted?

Yes. Every Body & Butter soap is handcrafted in small batches to ensure premium quality, freshness, and attention to detail.
